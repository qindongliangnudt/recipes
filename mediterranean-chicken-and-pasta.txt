Ingredients:
 - 16 - ounce jar marinated artichoke hearts
 - 1 tablespoon olive oil
 - 12 ounces skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces
 - 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
 - 1/4 cup chicken broth
 - 1/4 dry white wine
 - 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
 - 17 - ounce jar roasted red peppers, drained and cut into strips
 - 1/4 cup pitted kalamata olives
 - 3 cups hot cooked campanelle or penne pasta
 - 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

Directions:
 1. Drain artichoke hearts, reserving marinade, and chop them. 
 In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; add chicken and garlic. 
 Cook and stir until chicken is brown. 
 Add the reserved artichoke marinade, broth, wine and dried oregano.

  2. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Simmer, covered, 10 minutes. 
 Stir in chopped artichokes, roasted peppers and olives.

  3. To serve, spoon chicken mixture over pasta. 
 If desired, sprinkle with feta cheese.
